    LA Country RP (LACRP) was established in 2021 with the mission of creating a welcoming, exciting, and safe environment for all members. Powered by FiveM servers featuring custom-designed assets and scripts, we deliver a one-of-a-kind roleplaying experience. LACRP is a community where members can unwind, forge new friendships, or even spark rivalries. As a growing and evolving group, were thrilled about the opportunities ahead and cant wait to share them with you. Join us, and lets shape the future of LA Country RP together!
